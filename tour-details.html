Tour Overview

Begin your Kerala adventure in Munnar, a paradise of misty mountains and endless tea estates. Enjoy guided treks, plantation visits, and breathtaking viewpoints. Perfect for couples, families, and solo travelers seeking tranquility and natural beauty.

With DreamGuide Kerala, every detail is taken care of—from comfortable stays to authentic local experiences. Our expert guides ensure you discover the best of Munnar, from sunrise at Top Station to the aroma of fresh tea leaves.

Tour Highlights

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Munnar

Arrive in Munnar and check into your hotel. Enjoy a welcome dinner and relax amidst the cool mountain air.

Misty Mountain Resort Welcome Dinner
Day 2

Tea Plantation & Top Station

Guided walk through lush tea gardens, visit a tea factory, and trek to Top Station for panoramic views. Evening at leisure.

Misty Mountain Resort Breakfast, Lunch
Day 3

Eravikulam National Park & Departure

Morning visit to Eravikulam National Park to spot Nilgiri Tahr and enjoy the scenery. Transfer to your next destination or airport.
