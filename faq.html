What is DiscoverGuide Kerala?

DiscoverGuide Kerala is a trusted travel agency specializing in Kerala tours, holiday packages, and personalized travel experiences across God's Own Country.

Why should I book with DiscoverGuide?

We offer expert local knowledge, curated itineraries, reliable service, and 24/7 support to ensure your Kerala trip is memorable and hassle-free.

What destinations do you cover?

We cover all major destinations in Kerala including Munnar, Alleppey, Kochi, Wayanad, Thekkady, Kovalam, and more. Custom tours are available on request.

Is Kerala safe for tourists?

Kerala is one of India's safest and most welcoming states for tourists. Our team ensures your safety and comfort throughout your journey.

Do you offer support during the trip?

Yes, our support team is available 24/7 to assist you with any queries or emergencies during your Kerala tour.

How do I book a Kerala tour?

You can book online via our website, call us directly, or email your requirements. Our team will guide you through the booking process and help customize your itinerary.

Can I customize my tour package?

Absolutely! We offer fully customizable Kerala tour packages to suit your interests, budget, and travel dates.

What documents do I need to travel?

Indian citizens need valid ID proof. International travelers require a valid passport and visa. Our team will advise you on any additional requirements.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance, especially during peak seasons (Nov-Feb, Apr-May) for best availability and rates.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI, bank transfer, credit/debit cards, and cash. International payments can be made via wire transfer or PayPal.

Is my payment secure?

Yes, all payments are processed securely. We use trusted payment gateways and never share your financial information.

Can I get a refund if I cancel?

Refunds are available as per our cancellation policy. Please contact us for details on refunds and rescheduling options.
