Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, process, and disclose your information, including personal information, in conjunction with your access to and use of our services.

Privacy Policy

Discover Guide Travel Company is committed to protecting the privacy of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our services, including our website, mobile apps, and travel booking services.

1. Information We Collect:

  • Personal Information: Name, address, phone number, email address, date of birth, passport details, visa information, emergency contact information.
  • Travel Information: Flight details, hotel reservations, travel dates, destinations, travel preferences (e.g., seating, meal preferences), loyalty program numbers.
  • Payment Information: Credit card details, billing address, payment history.
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device information, cookies, website usage data.
  • Health Information: We may collect health information (e.g., allergies, medical conditions) only with your explicit consent, and only when necessary for specific trips, activities, or insurance purposes.
  • Information from Third Parties: We may receive information about you from third-party sources, such as booking platforms, airlines, hotels, and other travel partners, to facilitate your travel arrangements.

2. How We Use Your Information:

  • Providing Travel Services: Booking flights, hotels, tours, transportation (including arrangements you make with us), and other travel arrangements.
  • Customer Support: Responding to your inquiries, resolving issues, and providing assistance.
  • Marketing and Communication: Sending you promotional emails, newsletters, and special offers about our services (you can opt out of these communications at any time).
  • Improving Services: Analyzing data to understand customer preferences and improve our offerings, website, and apps.
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting legal and regulatory requirements.
  • Personalization: Tailoring travel recommendations and offers based on your past travel history and preferences.

3. Sharing Your Information:

  • Travel Partners: Airlines, hotels, tour operators, car rental companies, transportation providers, and other service providers necessary for fulfilling your travel arrangements.
  • Third-Party Service Providers: Companies that assist us with payment processing, IT services, marketing, customer support, and other essential services. We ensure these providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our legal rights, or in response to a legal request.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Data Security:

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. These measures include encryption, firewalls, and access controls. However, no data transmission over the internet is completely secure.

5. Data Retention:

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, or as required by law.

6. Children's Privacy:

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of five without parental consent. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Your Rights:

You may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Please contact us if you wish to exercise these rights.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy:

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on our website and encourage you to review it periodically.
